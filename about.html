About Me

My name is Justýna, I am 18 years old, and art represents one of the most important ways for me to express my emotions, thoughts, and unique perspective on the world. I am most inspired by nature, especially the sea, waves, and trees, whose ever-changing forms never cease to fascinate me – something you can notice in my paintings.

Painting is not only a passion for me, but also a form of relaxation and therapy. When I immerse myself in the creative process, I leave all worries behind and focus entirely on colors, shapes, and composition. Painting relieves my stress and brings me inner peace.

In addition to art, I am a student studying social sciences at high school, which often reflects in my work. I also draw inspiration from the nature surrounding my home in Březnice near Zlín, which surrounds me every day and motivates me to come up with new creative ideas.

For me, art is not only a means of self-expression but also an inexhaustible source of joy and inner fulfillment.
